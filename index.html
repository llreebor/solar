Got a Tech Problem?

Solar B.I. helps Solar Developers solve problems. Whether you have a big idea or a bottleneck, we aim to be your trusted technology partners to find your solution.

We bring our solar and software expertise to first understand your problem. Then we work with you to evaluate and tailor a solution. We love to build bespoke technology but we also have experience with many off-the-shelf tools and can advise your selection.

We’re with you every step of the way.

Our Favorite Projects

Sales Proposals
Our client’s sales team built and sent proposals to prospective clients. Each proposal could take up to an hour to create. The client evaluated the sales proposal tool offered by Helioscope (now Aurora), but they felt that their communications would be too constrained and the Helioscope templates wouldn’t allow them to communicate their competitive advantage. They asked Solar B.I. to build a bespoke tool to automate proposal generation.
